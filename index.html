About

Clinical Systems Analyst and Full Stack Web Developer/Designer. I have a diverse background in the health care industry ranging from direct patient care and managed care to healthcare IT. My new area of focus is on web development and design for the health care industry. I bring creativity, innovation and a focus to the ultimate goal of using technology to maximize health and wellbeing. As a Full Stack Web Developer/Designer I have found an effective and relevant venue to deliver needed content to health care providers and consumers..

My passion is creating relevant usable content for patient empowerment to achieve maximal health.
